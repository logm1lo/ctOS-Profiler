Welcome to the next evolution of personal security and surveillance. Inspired by Watch Dogs, ctOS - Profiler transforms your mobile device into a high-performance intelligence tool, capable of identifying and profiling targets in real-time.

Using AI and Computer Vision, ctOS - Profiler allows you to scan your surroundings, detect faces, and build a comprehensive intelligence database directly on your device.

KEY FEATURES:

Advanced Face Recognition
Utilizing LiteRT (TensorFlow Lite) and high-performance neural networks (FaceNet/MobileFaceNet), the profiler identifies and tracks individuals with surgical precision.

Deep Target Profiling
Generate detailed digital dossiers for any target. Customize and store data including:
* Demographics: Name, age, birth date (DMY format), and occupation.
* Biometrics: Track height (cm) and weight (kg) for accurate physical profiling.
* Socio-Economic Status: Assign income levels and social tiers.
* Risk Assessment: Calculate real-time threat levels based on behavioral parameters.
* Personality Profiling: Analyze and assign positive and negative personality traits to build a complete psychological profile.

Privacy-First & Offline
Intelligence shouldn't be shared. All processing happens locally on your hardware.
* No cloud uploads.
* Secure local SQLite database.
* On-device AI inference ensures your data stays in your hands.

TECHNICAL SPECS:
* Optimized for Android SDK 35.
* Powered by OpenCV for native camera frame processing.
* Highly efficient memory mapping for AI models, ensuring smooth performance even on mid-range devices.

DISCLAIMER:
ctOS - Profiler is a simulation and entertainment tool inspired by cyberpunk culture and hacker fiction. It is designed for roleplay and creative profiling; it does not access real-world government or private databases.
